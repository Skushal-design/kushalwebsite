About Me

Hello, I'm Kushal Sharma, a 3D Artist from Nepal with a passion for creating amazing worlds and Scene. I specialize in Shading and Lighting using popular tools like Autodesk Maya, 3ds Max, Blender, Unreal Engine, DaVinci Resolve, Adobe After Effects, and Premiere Pro to bring my ideas to life.

I’m always learning new techniques to create high-quality, cutting-edge content. Whether it’s CG lighting, logo animation, or motion graphics, I’m passionate about pushing my creativity and skills with each project.
